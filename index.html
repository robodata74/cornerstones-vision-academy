About Us

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) is committed to nurturing a generation equipped with essential knowledge, skills, values, and a mindset capable of navigating the complexities of contemporary society.

Our Vision

To become a lighthouse of the highest standard of education and extracurricular excellence.

Our Mission

To provide quality, values-driven education that develops academic excellence, character, leadership, and service.

Our Values

Integrity, respect, discipline, responsibility, and lifelong learning.

Academics
