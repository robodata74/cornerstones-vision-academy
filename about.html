About Cornerstones Vision Academy

Our Story

Our Story

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) was founded to provide a high-quality education that emphasizes academic excellence, cultural identity, and character development. Our mission is to empower young learners to thrive locally and globally.

From humble beginnings, CVA has grown into a leading institution in South Sudan, nurturing students to become confident, ethical, and innovative future leaders.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading institution in South Sudan that shapes empowered, culturally-aware, and academically-excellent leaders.

Mission

  • Deliver holistic education combining academic rigor with character development.
  • Foster cultural pride and community responsibility.
  • Provide a nurturing environment for creativity, leadership, and innovation.
